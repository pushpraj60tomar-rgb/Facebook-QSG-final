## Introduction
Facebook is a social networking platform that enables users to share text updates, photos, and videos with friends, followers, and selected audiences. Posting content helps users communicate updates, express ideas, and engage with others on the platform.

This guide provides clear, task-based instructions for creating posts using the Facebook mobile app and the Facebook website on a computer. It covers text-only posts as well as photo and video posts.

The guide also includes troubleshooting information and best practices to help users create and publish posts successfully.

## Before You Get Started
Before you create a post, ensure the following:

- Use a supported device: Android, iOS, or a computer with a web browser.
- Connect to a stable internet connection.
- Log in to your Facebook account. Create an account if you do not have one.

**Note:** You can create posts using either the mobile app or the desktop website.

## Create a Post Using the Mobile App
This section describes how to create and publish Facebook posts using the mobile app on Android and iOS devices. Use this section to post content quickly from a mobile device.

This section covers how to:
- Create a text-only post to share written updates.
- Create a photo or video post using media from your device.
---

### Create a Text-Only Post on Mobile

**To create a text-only post:**

1. On your mobile device, open the Facebook app.  
2. On the Home screen, tap **What’s on your mind?**  
3. In the post text box, enter your message.  
4. In the **Audience Selector**, choose who can view the post.  
5. (Optional) In the post options, tag people or add emojis.  
6. On the post screen, tap **Post**.  

Facebook publishes your text-only post to your selected audience. You can edit or delete it later.

---

### Create a Photo or Video Post on Mobile

**To create a photo or video post:**

1. On your mobile device, open the Facebook app.  
2. On the Home or Profile page, tap **What’s on your mind?**  
3. On the post screen, tap **Photo/Video**.  
4. From the device gallery, select one or more photos or videos.  
5. On the media preview screen, tap **Next** (Android) or **Done** (iOS).  
6. In the caption field, enter a description.  
7. (Optional) In the media options, add more photos or videos.  
8. In the **Audience Selector**, choose who can view the post.  
9. On the post screen, tap **Post**.  

Facebook uploads and publishes your media post.

## Create a Post Using a Computer (Web)
This section describes how to create and publish Facebook posts using the Facebook website in a web browser on a desktop or laptop computer.

This section covers how to:
- Create a text-only post from a web browser. 
- Create a photo or video post by uploading media from your computer.
---

### Create a Text-Only Post on Web or Computer

**To create a text-only post:**

1. In a web browser, open `https://www.facebook.com`.  
2. On the login page, sign in to your Facebook account.  
3. On the News Feed or Profile page, click **What’s on your mind?**  
4. In the post text box, enter your message.  
5. In the **Audience Selector**, choose who can view the post.  
6. (Optional) In the post options, tag friends or add emojis.  
7. At the bottom of the post window, click **Post**.  

Facebook publishes your post to your timeline.

---

### Create a Photo or Video Post on Web or Computer

**To create a photo or video post:**

1. In a web browser, open `https://www.facebook.com`.  
2. On the login page, sign in to your account.  
3. On the News Feed or Profile page, click **What’s on your mind?**  
4. Below the text box, click **Photo/Video**.  
5. In the file selection window, select one or more media files.  
6. In the caption field, enter a description.  
7. In the **Audience Selector**, choose who can view the post.  
8. At the bottom of the post window, click **Post**.  

Facebook uploads and shares your media post.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Do I need a Facebook account to create a post?**  
Yes. You must log in to publish any post.

**Q2. Can I save a post as a draft?**  
Yes. Facebook allows you to save drafts and access them later.

**Q3. Can I edit a post after publishing?**  
Yes. Select the three dots (⋯) on the post and choose **Edit Post**.

**Q4. How do I delete a post?**  
Open the post, select the three dots (⋯), and choose **Delete**.

**Q5. Can I schedule a post from the mobile app?**  
No. Scheduling is available through Meta Business Suite or desktop tools.

## Troubleshooting

### Issue 1: Cannot find the post creation option
- Ensure you are on the Home tab.
- Look for the **What’s on your mind?** box.
- Update the Facebook app or browser.

### Issue 2: Post does not publish
- Check your internet connection.
- Restart the app or browser.
- Clear cache or cookies.
- Update Facebook to the latest version.

### Issue 3: App or browser freezes
- Close and reopen Facebook.
- Restart your device.
- Update or reinstall the app.
